Let’s talk about accepting payments with Bāsys.

Processing Solutions

COUNTERTOP TERMINALS
We offer the latest countertop processing equipment supporting EMV and contactless payment functionality. Ethernet, dialup, and Wi-Fi connectivity are available.
MOBILE TERMINALS
Accept debit and credit card payments on a tablet, smartphone, or a fully dedicated wireless terminal, using a Wi-Fi or LTE connection.
VIRTUAL TERMINAL
Our virtual terminal, iQ Pro, is ideal for accepting payments over the phone, or B2B organizations seeking Level II and Level III rates. Options include recurring billing, emailing invoices, and more.
POINT OF SALE
For integrated business tools and an intuitive end-user experience, we offer the Clover® POS solution. The integrated apps and add-ons allow it to scale with your business for ultimate flexibility.
E-COMMERCE
Collect payments anytime, anywhere, by allowing your customers to pay online. We can implement a secure e-commerce solution for your business or integrate with an existing one.
SOFTWARE INTEGRATION
Process card payments securely without leaving your existing Point of Sale (POS) or Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. We can integrate with almost any system currently on the market.
